About me I was a pencil sketch artist in the 80,90s I drew over 10,000 Illustrations for anime fans for CFO “cartoon fantasy origination” every where. I tried  to race in the suppercross  with my 2 84 RM Suzuki 125 race bikes in 85 to 89 I raced  in Riverside CA A few times but mostly it was for fun.. I have a lot of knowledge in auto mechanics I worked on funny cars in high school and ROP classes for years before I attended college and achieved a certificate in electronics with highest honors. I still go to college from time to time to get my AS and more certificates. Tried to be a professional body builder but never really competed. I worked as a ed artist for a few years in the 90s and that what I still do from time to time and still my favorite hobby. I studied computers my whole life since the commodore 64 days I even still have a working commodore-sx64
and that’s what I do now for a living.
